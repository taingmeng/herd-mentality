# OBJECT OF THE GAME

**{{ GAME_NAME }}** is a drawing and guessing party game.

Players take turns as the **guesser** while everyone else is a **sketcher**. Score the most points by drawing well and guessing correctly.

## SETUP

Enter all player names and choose the number of rounds (each player will be the guesser once per round).

You will need **paper and markers/pens** for drawing.

## HOW TO PLAY

1. **Pass the device** — Each sketcher taps to secretly see their unique **descriptor + subject** pair (e.g. "dancing bear" or "angry cyclist"). Don't let anyone else see!

2. **Draw** — Sketchers draw their word combo on paper. No letters or numbers allowed!

3. **Guesser looks** — The guesser sees all the assigned descriptors and subjects, plus some **decoy words** mixed in. Try to match each drawing to the correct descriptor + subject pair.

## SCORING

After the guesser has made their guesses, score each sketcher:

- **0** — Guesser didn't match either word. Sketcher gets **0 pts**, guesser gets **0 pts**.
- **1** — Guesser matched partially (one word correct). Sketcher gets **1 pt**, guesser gets **0 pts**.
- **2** — Guesser matched both words perfectly. Sketcher gets **3 pts**, guesser gets **2 pts**.

## WINNING

After all rounds are complete, the player with the most points wins!
