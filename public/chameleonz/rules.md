# OBJECT OF THE GAME

**{{ GAME_NAME }}** is a social deduction game.

A category and a 4x4 grid of words are shown. One player is secretly the Chameleon — they can see the grid but don't know which word is the secret word.

All other players know the secret word (highlighted on the grid).

Each player gives a one-word clue related to the secret word. The Chameleon must try to blend in without knowing the word.

After all clues are given, count down 3, 2, 1 and everyone points to who they think the Chameleon is.

If the Chameleon is caught, they get one chance to guess the secret word. If they guess correctly, the Chameleon wins. Otherwise, the other players win.

If the Chameleon is not caught (no clear majority), the Chameleon wins.
