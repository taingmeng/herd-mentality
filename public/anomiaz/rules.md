# {{ GAME_NAME }}

## Overview

{{ GAME_NAME }} is a fast-paced card game where players are dealt cards with categories and colored symbols. When symbols match, a face-off begins!

## Setup

1. Enter at least 3 player names (one per line).
2. Choose the number of cards in the deck (100-500).
3. Press **Start Game**.

## How to Play

- Press **Deal** to give the current player a card from the deck.
- Each category card has a **category** (e.g. "A type of dog breed") and a **colored symbol**.
- Play proceeds clockwise.

## Face-Offs

- **Direct Match:** If two players have the same symbol on their top card, a face-off is triggered.
- **Wild Match:** Wild cards show two symbols. If one player's top card matches one symbol and another player's top card matches the other, it is a face-off.
- During a face-off, each player says an answer for their category. The group decides who gave the **worse** answer.
- **Tap the losing player's card** to remove it. The other player earns **+1 point**.
- After a face-off resolves, check for cascading matches from newly revealed top cards.

## Wild Cards

- Wild cards are placed in the center when dealt.
- They display two different colored symbols.
- They create matches between players who have those two symbols on their top cards.

## Winning

- The game ends when all cards have been dealt and all face-offs are resolved.
- The player with the most points wins!
