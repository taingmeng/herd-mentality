## Setup

- Split into two teams. One device is needed to run the game.
- Choose a game mode: **Random Category** (each round uses one category) or **All Categories** (words from any category).
- Set a target score. The first team to reach the target wins.

## How to play

Each round lasts **60 seconds**. One player from the active team holds the device and makes sounds and movements to get their team to guess the word on screen.

**Important:** Your hands must be behind your back! Use only sounds and body movements to communicate.

## Scoring

- Press **Correct (+1)** when your team guesses the word.
- Press **Skip (-1)** to skip a difficult word (costs one point).
- Try to get through as many words as possible before time runs out.

## Round end

After 60 seconds, the round ends. Review the words guessed and the round score. If the total score reaches the target, the game ends. Otherwise, the next team takes a turn.

## Winning

The first team to reach the target score wins the game!
